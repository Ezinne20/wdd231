Security Tips

Follow these tips to safeguard your personal information:

Use Strong Passwords

Ensure your passwords are long, unique, and contain a mix of characters.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Always activate 2FA for added security when possible.

Be Cautious of Phishing

Don’t click on suspicious links or share sensitive information with unknown sources.

Keep Software Up-to-Date

Ensure your operating system and software are regularly updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
